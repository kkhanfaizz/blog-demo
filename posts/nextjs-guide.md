## Why Next.js?
Next.js aapko out-of-the-box features deta hai:

- **Server-Side Rendering (SSR)**
- **Static Site Generation (SSG)**
- **API Routes**
- **Image Optimization**

Agar aap React developer ho to Next.js seekhna bahut easy hai.
